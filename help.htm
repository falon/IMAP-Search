IMAP Headers Fetch

Abstract

This tool allows to look for the IMAP UID or an header and show headers of found emails.

Procedure

You must provide:

  1. username (a kind of <name.surname>@<domain>)
  2. the folder where mail you believe to be.
    1. You must follow your server namespace.
      Ie: INBOX/Trash or INBOX.Trash or Trash too.
    2. Take care on case: IMAP folder names are case sensitive.
      The folder Archive is different from folder archive.
  3. The IMAP UID or the Message-ID of the mail.

Insert that data, then click on "View Header".

Example

You find an IMAP uid, folder and username looking for the following keys in Open-Xchange logs:


ERROR Attachment not found

or

ERROR Attachment not found <username>
